Video QoE Subjective Survey

Thanks for agreeing to take part in this survey. The aim of this survey is to measure your perception of the overall quality of the videos that will be shown to you. The videos have one or more impairments like stalls, sounds quality issue, delays and picture quality issues. So pick a rating that accurately reflect how you felt about the sound and image quality of the videos that will be shown to you. The survey only takes about 3 mins 30 seconds to complete.

Before you begin, you will be asked to complete a very short questionaire (with two questions) about yourself. Please click next when you are ready to start.
